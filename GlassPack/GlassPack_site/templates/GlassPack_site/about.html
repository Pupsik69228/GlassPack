About us

GlassPack is a major wholesale supplier of glass bottles and jars for a wide
range of industries across the European market. The company has been
operating since 2009, providing clients with stable and high-quality service.

Over the years, we have proven to be a reliable partner for manufacturers of
spirits, wine, beer, gourmet foods, cosmetics, and beekeeping products
— offering both standard and custom-designed glass packaging solutions.

Our core strength lies in delivering products that combine visual appeal,
durability, and competitive pricing. We ensure that our packaging fully
meets client expectations while complying with international quality and
safety standards.

We have developed long-standing relationships with highly professional
and certified glass manufacturing partners. This enables us to supply
environmentally friendly and high-quality containers at a fair and
consistent price.

Our team is ready to support clients through every stage of product
development — from the initial concept and technical drawings to the
final delivery. We offer complete guidance and production expertise
tailored to the customer’s needs.

At GlassPack, we operate on the principles of consistency, flexibility,
precision, openness, and long-term cooperation built on mutual benefit.

GlassPack – your reliable partner in premium glass packaging!
